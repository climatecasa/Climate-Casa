Climate Casa

House of Climate Positive Projects

Climate Casa began with a simple purpose: to educate people about climate and sustainability.

It is a warm, home-like space where storytelling and nature-inspired art bring environmental ideas closer to people in a creative and meaningful way.

A casa where you can pause for eco-adventures, create with nature, and walk away feeling a little more connected to the world around you. 🌿

~ Himanshi Shrimali, Founder

Art Shelves

A curated collection of handmade art crafted from ethically sourced fallen twigs, leaves, petals, and repurposed paper bags. Every piece is mindfully handcrafted for gifting and life’s special moments.
